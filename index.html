Our Mission

Due to the large amount of capital and long investment horizons required in order to pursue their strategies, Private Equity have traditionally been exclusive investment opportunities offered to high net worth individuals, corporations, and asset managers. The average retail investor seeking to add private companies to their portfolio is generally unable to do so without proper connections in place, resulting in a large capital pool left untapped or invested into more accessible investment vehicles.

FiDi Capital seeks to connect Private Equity Funds managers and retail investors by using blockchain Technology to tokenize investment into a fund into “shares” that can be bought and traded.
